Frequently Asked Questions

Can the AI sync with multiple calendars and team availability slots? +

Yes. The AI connects directly to Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly, or custom ERP schedulers. It reads real-time availability across team members and round-robins meetings appropriately.

How does it handle rescheduling or cancellations? +

If a user requests a change via chat or email, the AI identifies their original slot, checks new availabilities, edits the database log, and updates calendar entries automatically, sending a new WhatsApp confirmation.

Can it process booking fees or deposits? +

Yes. We can integrate payment gateways (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal). Once a slot is selected, the AI generates a payment link, holds the slot, and confirms the booking only after payment verification.
