What is an AI automation agency? +

An AI automation agency builds custom AI systems — chatbots, WhatsApp agents, voice agents, and workflow automation — that replace manual, repetitive tasks in your business. This saves time, reduces costs, and lets your team focus on high-value work. Automyze is a full-service AI automation agency based in Mumbai, India.

How much does AI automation cost for a small business in India? +

At Automyze, AI automation projects for small businesses in India typically start from ₹15,000 depending on scope and complexity. Most projects are completed within 2–4 weeks. We offer a free 30-minute discovery call to assess your specific needs before any commitment.

What is a WhatsApp AI agent and how does it help my business? +

A WhatsApp AI agent automatically responds to your customers on WhatsApp 24/7 — qualifying leads, answering queries, and booking appointments with zero human involvement. With over 500 million WhatsApp users in India, this is one of the highest-ROI automations for Indian businesses. Automyze builds custom WhatsApp AI agents integrated with your CRM and workflows.

What is the difference between an inbound and outbound voice agent? +

An inbound voice agent answers incoming calls automatically — handling queries and booking meetings like a trained receptionist, available 24/7. An outbound voice agent proactively calls your lead list, qualifies prospects, and passes only sales-ready conversations to your human team. Automyze builds both types for businesses across India.

How long does it take to build and deploy an AI system? +

Automyze delivers most AI automation systems within 2–4 weeks. The process: discovery call → custom build → integration with your existing tools (CRM, WhatsApp, website) → live launch with full handover and documentation.

Do I need a technical team to use your AI systems? +

No. We handle the entire build and integration. Once your system is live, we provide full documentation and a handover session so your existing team can manage it without any technical knowledge. Most of our clients have zero technical background.

Will the AI integrate with tools I already use? +

Yes. We integrate with most popular business tools including WhatsApp Business, CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho), Google Workspace, Tally, Calendly, and more. If you use a tool, we can almost certainly connect to it.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake? +

All our systems are built with human escalation built-in. If the AI is uncertain or the query is complex, it automatically routes to a human. Our RAG chatbots have 98% accuracy in production. We also provide 30 days of post-launch monitoring to catch and fix any edge cases.

Can you automate my WhatsApp Business account? +

Yes — this is one of our most popular services for Indian businesses. We integrate AI agents with WhatsApp Business API to handle lead qualification, FAQ responses, appointment booking, and follow-ups automatically. With 500M+ WhatsApp users in India, this is one of the highest-ROI automations we offer.

Is my business data safe? +

Absolutely. We follow strict data privacy practices. Your business data used to train AI systems is never shared with third parties. All systems are built with security-first architecture and we can sign NDAs before any project begins.
